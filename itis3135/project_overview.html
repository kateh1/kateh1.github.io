Project Name:

Excalibur Tees Website

Project overview:

  • Purpose: Provide a homebase for Excalibur Tees. Potential clients will be pointed towards this site to learn more about the business and how to order.
  • Intended Users: Excalibur Tees is a business that creates discounted tee shirts and other various customized merchandise. They target K-12 education programs, specifically competitive robotics teams, to help supply team merchandise at an affordable price. The site will be designed with their potential clients in mind.
  • Website Content: Displaying the products avaiable for order will be the main focus of the site. The site will also introduce the owners and the goal of the company. Finally, users will be able to contact the business through the site to get a quote.
