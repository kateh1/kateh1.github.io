How to Place an Order

Ordering custom T-shirts with Excalibur Tees is simple and hassle-free. Follow the steps below to get started and bring your vision to life!

Step 1: Submit Your Design

Share your ideas with us! You can upload your own design or work with our team to create one. Visit our Contact Us page to send us your design or reach out for design assistance.

Step 2: Choose Your Options

Customize your T-shirts to fit your needs:

  • Sizes: Youth and adult sizes available.
  • Colors: Select from a variety of vibrant and neutral tones.
  • Printing Styles: Choose between screen printing, heat transfer, or embroidery.
  • Quantities: Small or large orders—we’ve got you covered!

Step 3: Get a Quote

Once we receive your design and options, we’ll provide a detailed quote, including pricing, production time, and delivery options. We offer discounts for bulk orders and educational programs!

Step 4: Approve and Pay

Review your quote and confirm your order. Payment options include credit card, purchase orders for schools, or direct invoicing. Once payment is received, we’ll start production.

Step 5: Receive Your Order

Your custom T-shirts will be delivered on time and ready to impress! We offer shipping and local pickup options for your convenience.

Need Assistance?

If you have any questions about the ordering process, feel free to reach out through our Contact Us page. We’re here to help every step of the way.
