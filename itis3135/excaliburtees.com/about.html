About Us

At Excalibur Tees, we’re passionate about helping K-12 educational programs shine. Based on years of experience in custom apparel, we’ve built a reputation for delivering high-quality, affordable T-shirts tailored to the unique needs of schools, classrooms, and extracurricular programs.

Our Mission

Our Mission: To empower schools and students by creating T-shirts that inspire unity, pride, and unforgettable memories.

Our Story

Excalibur Tees was founded with a simple idea: to make custom T-shirts accessible and fun for K-12 programs. As former educators and parents, we understand how important it is to celebrate school spirit, teamwork, and achievements. From our first order for a local robotics team to serving schools nationwide, we’ve grown into a trusted partner for schools everywhere.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Dedicated to Schools: We exclusively work with K-12 programs, so we understand your needs.
  • Customizable Options: From school mascots to event logos, we bring your ideas to life.
  • Fast, Reliable Service: We know deadlines matter, and we’re committed to delivering on time, every time.

Our Commitment to Quality

Every T-shirt we create is made with care using top-quality materials and printing techniques. We’re committed to delivering products that students, teachers, and parents will love.

Giving Back

We’re proud to give back by offering discounts for Title I schools and donating a portion of our profits to educational initiatives.
